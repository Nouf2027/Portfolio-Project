# Masar - مسار 🚀

> A platform that helps Saudi newcomers in tech discover their ideal career path

---

## 📌 About The Project

**Masar** is a web platform designed to help fresh graduates and anyone interested in entering the tech field for the first time find their ideal technical career path. Through a simple questionnaire, the platform recommends the most suitable path and provides a detailed roadmap including required skills, tools, job titles, and professional certifications — all tailored for the Saudi market.

### The Problem We Solve
- Anyone entering tech doesn't know where to start: Frontend? Backend? DevOps? Data Science? 
- Most existing resources are in English and not tailored to the Saudi market
- No Arabic community platform specialized for Saudi tech enthusiasts
- Previous software engineering graduates faced career shock after specializing

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

## ⚡ Challenges & Opportunities

### Potential Challenges
- Takes time to gain user trust and adoption
- Data requires continuous updates to stay relevant
- Needs customization to reflect the Saudi job market accurately

### Opportunities Identified During Brainstorming
- No direct Arabic competitor focused on Saudi tech newcomers
- Growing Saudi tech market under Vision 2030
- High demand for tech talent in Saudi Arabia
- Previous software engineering cohorts faced career shock after specializing — Masar addresses this gap

---
## ❌ Rejected Ideas

### 1. RequestFlow - Internal Request Management System
A web-based internal system for startup employees to submit requests (leave, equipment, supplies) electronically instead of WhatsApp and email.

**Reason for Rejection:**
- Already exists in the market (SAP, Monday, Jira)
- Insufficient competitive advantage
- Limited target market

### 2. Problem-Solving Marketplace
A platform where small businesses post their problems and users submit solutions.

**Reason for Rejection:**
- Difficult to verify quality of submitted solutions
- Requires large user base from the start
- Implementation may exceed project timeline
